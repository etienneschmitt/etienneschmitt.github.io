I am a R&D engineer working at Inria Strasbourg, in the Mimesis team. Our team is specialized in real-time medical simulation. Based on the SOFA framework, we develop innovative technologies to train, assist and guide clinicians. My work in focused on topology data structures. I am involved in the rewriting of the CGoGN library, a c++11 implementation of combinatorial maps. I am currently working on using CGoGN as the default topology model in SOFA.
